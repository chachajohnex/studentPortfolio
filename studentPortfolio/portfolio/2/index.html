On the non-skilled side, one crucial trait I have cultivated is adaptability. Whether navigating new academic challenges or adjusting to the dynamic environment of a construction site, being adaptable has been essential. This flexibility allows me to quickly learn and adapt to new situations, enhancing my effectiveness in both academic and professional settings.

Additionally, I have taken part in sports and other recreational activities, fostering a well-rounded approach to personal development. Overall, my journey thus far has been marked by a commitment to learning, growth, and a holistic approach to education and personal development.
